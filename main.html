BIOGRAPHY

Riley Aaron is a Louisville-based artist with a talent for melodic-driven vocals that glide over dusty guitar and hip-hop isntrumentals. His music is "home-grown"- acting as producer, songwriter, and audio engineer, Riley always has his hand in the recording process. Even when he had traveled to Chicago to record in Classick Studios, working with high-level engineers, the same engineers who have previously tweaked the sounds from artists such as Pheolix and Taylor Bennett.

After recording in Chicago in 2019, Riley has followed up in 2020 with two collaborative singles; "Rain" with Jay Squared, and "Vacant" with Michael Christmas. These two collaborations have given a new surge to Riley's fanbase.
